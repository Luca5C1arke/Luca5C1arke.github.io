Q: Has JZMotors won any awards?


A: As one of the top used car dealers in Melbourne, JZ Motors received the runner up position in the 2009 Carsales Dealer of the Year Awards for the state of Victoria. We qualified for the award through Carsales Network’s customer surveys gathered within a span of two weeks, and the results were tallied according to specific criteria such as average dealer response time, ‘customers contacted’ ratio and closing ratio, just to name a few. We also won the 1979 Mazda Dealer of the Year Award!

Q: What inspections do your cars undergo?


A: Every used car, SUV, 4WD or commercial vehicle undergoes  a strict 120 point mechanical, body and safety inspection  and are serviced before delivery, the last thing we want to do is sell you a lemon. Once we are satisfied with the vehicle, the vehicle is prepared for sale.

Q: What warranties are included when purchasing?


A: We give you an Integrity 1 Year Extended Warranty for FREE (valued at $423.50) All our vehicles come with the usual Government Statutory Warranty, but we wanted to go one step further and provide you with an exclusive nationwide 1 Year Warranty. We also provide a Pre-Owned Vehicle 7-Day Exchange Guarantee. PRICELESS!! and just to make sure that you are really happy with your purchase, we have our 7-Day Exchange program.

Q: Do I have to come in-store to pickup?


A: We can arrange and ship used cars for all areas of Australia - just contact us for an exact shipping quote and we, as auto traders, can offer you very competitive and reasonable shipping cost.
